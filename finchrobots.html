Finch Robots

When working with remote control robots, students can learn the dynamics of collisions, safety and proper handling on assistive device such as a power wheelchair.

Collision can be very useful in Boccia because it could put the game in your favor.

In power wheelchair soccer, The wheelchair can collide with the soccer ball but the players are not allowed to collide with each other for safety reasons.
