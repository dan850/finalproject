CODE.ORG

Code.org uses interactive blocks to build small programs and exposes students of the step-by-step process, trial and error along with repetition. These process can translate over to physical sports.

When training for Power Wheelchair Soccer, players practice moving the wheelchair around cones in a repetitive manner.

Boccia players practice under obstacles to work on precision and accuracy.
